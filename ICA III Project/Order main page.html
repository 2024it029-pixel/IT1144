How to Order

Follow these simple steps to get your favorite meal ready:

Step 1: Visit our Menu Page and select the items you would like to have.

Step 2: Fill in your details next page, including your name, student ID, and the quantity.

Step 3: Mention any special requests, such as "less spicy" or "no onion," in the comments section.

Step 4: Click the 'Confirm Order' button to submit your request. You will receive a notification once it is prepared.
